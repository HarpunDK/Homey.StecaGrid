This app supports StecaGrid coolConcept Inverter on local LAN/connected.
Tested on StecaGrid 6003. I dont know the excact software-version of the inverter. 

The app will pull for values every one minute. Production, Temperature and Voltage is read.

Please visit the settings page, after installing this application. Configuration-fields are mandatory. 

If you like the app, please hit the "donate" button in the top of the page.
